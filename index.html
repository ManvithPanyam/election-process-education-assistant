Step by Step

How the Election Process Works

Follow the complete journey from start to finish. Click any stage to learn more.

📝

Voter Registration

Your name must appear on the electoral roll before you can vote.

✅
Who can register?

Any Indian citizen aged 18 or older on January 1 of the qualifying year.

📄
How to register

Fill Form 6 online at the NVSP portal or at your nearest Electoral Registration Office.

🪪
What you receive

Your EPIC (Voter ID card) — your official identity as a registered voter.

🎖️

Candidate Nominations

Candidates file nomination papers with the Returning Officer to officially enter the contest.

📋
Filing

Candidates submit forms, a security deposit, and proof of eligibility before the deadline.

🔍
Scrutiny

The Returning Officer verifies all nominations meet eligibility requirements.

🔙
Withdrawal

Candidates get a final window to withdraw before the contest is finalized.

📢

Campaign Period

Parties share their manifesto and reach out to voters under strict Election Commission rules.

📣
Campaign activities

Rallies, advertisements, pamphlets, and door-to-door outreach to seek votes.

⏰
Silence period

All campaigning stops 48 hours before polling. This lets voters reflect without pressure.

📜
Model Code of Conduct

No hate speech, bribery, or misuse of government resources during elections.

🗳️

Polling Day

The day you cast your vote using an Electronic Voting Machine (EVM).

🏫
At the polling booth

Your name must appear on the voter list (electoral roll) to vote. Carry your Voter ID or any approved photo ID for identity verification.

👆
Casting your vote

Press the button next to your chosen candidate. The VVPAT slip confirms your choice.

💜
The ink mark

Indelible ink on your left index finger prevents double voting. It's a badge of participation!

🔢

Vote Counting

After polling ends, sealed EVMs are opened on counting day under strict supervision.

🏢
Counting centers

Votes are tallied round by round, each covering a set of polling stations.

👁️
Transparency

Candidate agents observe the process. VVPAT slips are cross-verified from random booths.

📊
Live trends

Results are shared publicly in real time as counting progresses.

🏆

Result Declaration

The candidate with the most votes in each constituency wins the seat.

🎉
Winner declared

The Returning Officer announces results. They are published on the ECI website.

🏛️
Government formation

The majority party or coalition forms the government and selects its leader.

⚖️
Challenging results

Candidates can file an election petition in the High Court if irregularities are suspected.
